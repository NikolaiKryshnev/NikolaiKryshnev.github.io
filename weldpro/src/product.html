The TORROS MMA 250 (A2501) welding inverter (made in China) is designed for welding of different metals (low-carbon steels, stainless steels, copper, bronze, aluminium, cast iron ...) with the corresponding coated electrodes in the MMA mode. Thanks to the inverter technology the machine is lightweight, compact and low-consumption of mains power. Electrical diagram based on IGBT-transistors provides easy ignition of the electrode, its soft and stable burning without splashing, which positively affects the formation of weld and its strength. Welding current control is stepless, available from 30A to 250A, which accepts welding electrodes with diameters from 1.6 mm to 5.0 mm. The welding current is displayed digitally. The "ARC FORCE" mode - (arc boosting) mode helps to achieve deeper penetration of the workpiece with a short arc, without the risk of electrode sticking.
