About Us

Weldpro is a private company with a rich history. We specialize in the welding industry, including producing of welding materials and welding components. The history of the company has began in 1996 in Belarus, with a small private business owned and performed by one person. Today we sell our equipment and services in six European countries, and do not stop growing and developing.

OPENNESS

Openness and transparency are our core values and the basis of our relationships with the client. We are always happy to help with the selection or with a good piece of advice. RESPONSIBILITY

We have our own service center, which provides warranty and post-warranty service of the equipment bought from us. There, all welding machines are tested and prepared before sending to the buyer. There, all samples of new equipment are tested, and only after that procedure we make a decision to expand our product range with the best of them.
